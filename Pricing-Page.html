Paint Correction

One-Step Paint Correction

  • Starting at: $349
  • Full hand wash
  • Clay bar decontamination
  • One-step machine polish
  • Tires dressed
  • Final paint inspection

Best for: Light swirl marks and restoring basic gloss.

Restore your vehicle's shine with our One-Step Paint Correction package. This service includes a full hand wash, clay bar decontamination, and a one-step machine polish to remove light swirl marks and haze. Perfect for cars that need a quick refresh and enhanced gloss. Tires are dressed and a final paint inspection ensures quality results. Ideal for lightly worn paint looking to regain its factory shine.

Two-Step Paint Correction

  • Starting at: $649
  • Full hand wash
  • Clay bar decontamination
  • Compound (cutting stage)
  • Polish (refining stage)
  • Tires dressed
  • Final paint inspection

Best for: Moderate to heavy swirl marks, oxidation, and dull paint.

For vehicles with moderate to heavy swirl marks, oxidation, or dull paint, our Two-Step Paint Correction package offers a deeper restoration. This comprehensive service includes full hand washing, clay bar decontamination, a compound cutting stage followed by a polishing stage, and tire dressing. The process significantly improves clarity and gloss, revitalizing your vehicle's paint to near-new condition. A final paint inspection guarantees the best possible finish.
